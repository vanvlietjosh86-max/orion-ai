# PROJECT ORION

## Intelligent Investing Companion

## Motto

> Simple enough to understand. Smart enough to trust.

---

# Mission

Project ORION exists to help ordinary people become confident, knowledgeable, and disciplined investors through transparency, education, and thoughtful use of AI.

ORION is not designed to promise guaranteed profits.

ORION is designed to help users understand opportunities, manage risk, and make informed long-term decisions.

---

# Vision

To create a calm, intelligent investing companion that helps users build financial knowledge and develop better investing habits over time.

---

# Core Philosophy

## Understand before investing

Every recommendation should be explained clearly.

## Protect capital first

Risk management is more important than chasing short-term gains.

## Long-term thinking

ORION focuses on consistency, patience, and the power of compounding.

## Transparency

ORION explains why it reaches a conclusion and acknowledges uncertainty.

## Continuous learning

Every interaction should help the user become a better investor.
